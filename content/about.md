## Hey, we are Bear and Mouse 👋

When I (Bear) moved close to Geneva I started to do outdoors sports. I have become passionate about them! I like climbing (I am a beginne), hiking, camping and I like to take pictures. One thing I am really obsessed about is via ferratas (klettersteig)!

This blog is where I document my trips - the via ferratas, the hiking trails, how I reached there, and everything in between. Just honest stories from the mountains and with this I have some hope that my writing will improve.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Trip reports** from hikes and climbs across Europe and beyond
- **Gear reviews** from someone who actually breaks things
- **Practical guides** for planning your own adventures
- **Photography** from places that we visited
