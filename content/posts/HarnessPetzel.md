I am very happy with my Harness from Petzl Corax. I bought it in 2023 when I started doing via ferratas and have givin it a good used since then. [Find more about it in the Petzl site](https://www.petzl.com/INT/en/Sport/Harnesses/CORAX).


This harness fits me very well I have size 1. I like how it adjusts on the legs and on the hip. On the legs you can adjust the size of your leg but also the lenght from the hip staper until the leg staper. The foam on the leg makes it confortable when hanging. On the hips there are two (on each side) strapers to adjust. After 3 years I do not see a lot of hearing off of the materials. For me it has enough gear loops (4). And for sure I would by this model again.
