The 3 parts Via Ferrata of Parc Thermal is located close to Saint-Gervais-les-Bains in France. It is a very nice via ferrata that I would for sure go back again and do the last missing part! 

## Where to park

We went by car and there is a free nice parking just close to the via ferrata which makes it very easy to access.

## Approach and Descent

The approch is 10 minutes away and to descend it takes about 20 minutes and it is accessible.

## The Route

The first part is accessible and easy, in the second the difficulty increases and the last one which we did not do is supposed to be harder or has hard as the second one. I do not fully remember how long it took us to do. The were some ladders and bridges that we had to cross.

## Gear I Relied On

For this via ferrata I took my own equipament

- **Shoes**: Columbia Peakfreak OutDry shoes
- **Via Ferrata Kit**: Petzl Scorpio Vertigo
- **Harness**: Petzl Corax

## Practical Info

- **Best season**: July to September
- **Getting there**: By car
- **Site to find more information**: [Parc Thermal on Ferrata Guide](https://ferrataguide.com/ferrata/via_ferrata_du_parc_thermal)
