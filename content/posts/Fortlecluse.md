The via ferrata of Fort l’Ecluse was my first via ferrata ever so it has a special place in my heart! I imediatly got hooked was also where I meet Bear for the firt time.

## Where to park

We went by car and there is a free nice parking just close to the via ferrata which makes it very easy to access.

## The approch and descend

The approch is very easy and takes 10 minutes to reach. To descend it takes about 30 minutes and it is accesible.

## The Route

In ascend it takes 1h to do. The first time that I did it for sure it took us longer. There is nice places to rest you will find some rope briges to cross as well as a wood one. There is a part that is sigly overhand but overall the via ferrata is very nice and not too hard. 

## Gear I Relied On

The first time I did it I rent the via ferrata kit on cortigrimpe in Saint-Genis-Pouly (https://cortigrimpe01.fr/en/#accueil) everything was 20€.

- **Shoes**: I have to say I took my normal exercise shoes (which can be not a good idea) but since them I update it for my Columbia Peakfreak OutDry shoes.
- **Via Ferrata Kit**: Edelrid

## Practical Info

- **Best season**: July to September
- **Getting there**: By car
- **Site to find more information**: https://ferrataguide.com/ferrata/du_Fort_l%27Ecluse
