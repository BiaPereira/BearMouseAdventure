The via ferrata of Fort l’Ecluse was my first via ferrata ever so it has a special place in my heart! I immediately got hooked was also where I met Bear for the first time.

The view to the Rhône is amazing! From time to time a train will pass which makes the via ferrata even more epic.

## Where to park

We went by car and there is a free nice parking close to the via ferrata which makes it very easy to access.

## Approach and Descent

The approach is very easy and takes 10 minutes to reach. To descend it takes about 30 minutes and it is accessible.

## The Route

The ascent it takes 1h to do. The first time that I did it for sure it took us longer. There is nice places to rest you will find some rope bridges to cross as well as a wood one. There is a part that is slightly overhanging but overall the via ferrata is very nice and not too hard. 

## Gear I Relied On

The first time I did it I rented the via ferrata kit at [Cortigrimpe](https://cortigrimpe01.fr/en/#accueil) in Saint-Genis-Pouly everything was 20€.

- **Shoes**: I have to say I took my normal exercise shoes (may not be a good idea) but since then I updated it for my Columbia Peakfreak OutDry shoes.
- **Via Ferrata Kit**: Edelrid

## Practical Info

- **Best season**: July to September
- **Getting there**: By car
- **Site to find more information**: [Fort l'Ecluse on Ferrata Guide](https://ferrataguide.com/ferrata/du_Fort_l%27Ecluse)
